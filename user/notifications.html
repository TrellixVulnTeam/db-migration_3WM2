Notifications¶

The database migration processes will send notifications to the #wb-db-migration-events channel (Slack team: wormbase-db-dev).

Notifications are sent before and after each build step; at the end of the process a notification will sent to confirm the migrated Datomic databases’ location on Amazon S3 storage To configure notifications for you and the WormBase team:

Enter the slack webhook url to the following command:

azanium configure <SLACK_WEBHOOK_URL>
